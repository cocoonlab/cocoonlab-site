Partners

Research and design partners around Cocoon

Cocoon Lab builds Cocoon in dialogue with research institutions and design practices that keep the product grounded in architectural judgment, responsible AI, and real project conditions.

Company: Cocoon Lab Product: Cocoon Research, design, and civic alignment
Why partnership matters

Useful systems need research depth and practice feedback.

Cocoon is meant to support the earliest stages of architecture, where uncertainty is high and judgment matters most. That makes the quality of our partnerships especially important.

The network around Cocoon Lab helps keep the product both rigorous and relevant: close to research, close to practice, and close to the realities of planning, feasibility, and design authorship.

Relationship model

One core partner. Two design partners.

  • Mila helps anchor research depth, technical rigor, and responsible AI thinking.
  • Civiliti brings civic and urban systems perspective to early-stage decision-making.
  • Sid Lee Architecture keeps Cocoon connected to design practice and architectural judgment.
What this supports

Clearer product direction for Cocoon.

These relationships strengthen how Cocoon handles site, cost, carbon, regulatory, and visual signals before design is fixed.

The goal is not automation for its own sake. It is a product that helps teams understand context, reveal constraints, and move toward better decisions earlier.
