Partners around Cocoon Lab and Cocoon

Company: Cocoon Lab Product: Cocoon Research, design, and civic alignment

Cocoon Lab grows in dialogue with partners across research, design, and the built environment. These relationships help shape how Cocoon develops and where it can be useful.

Why partners matter

They sharpen the way Cocoon connects research depth, architectural practice, and real-world decision-making.

What the network supports

Responsible AI, design intelligence, and stronger alignment between research, practice, and the realities of early-stage work.

Where it leads

Stronger foundations for Cocoon as a product built to support architects, developers, and multidisciplinary teams with clarity.

Design Partner

Sid Lee Architecture brings practice-based design insight that keeps Cocoon connected to authorship, architectural judgment, and the real needs of early-stage workflow.

What these relationships support

Together, these partnerships help Cocoon Lab build a product that is technically informed, practice-aware, and grounded in better architectural decision-making rather than automation for its own sake.

How this connects to Cocoon

Cocoon is designed to make site, cost, carbon, code, and visual signals clearer before design is fixed. Partner insight helps keep that work relevant, disciplined, and useful.
