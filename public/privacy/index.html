Privacy for Cocoon Lab and Cocoon

Last updated: April 13, 2026 Contact: rashid@cocoonlab.ai Scope: cocoonlab.ai

Cocoon Lab operates this website and is the company behind Cocoon. This page explains what information we may receive through the site, how we use it, and how we handle communication when someone explores the product, requests a demo, or starts a business conversation.

What this covers

Website inquiries, demo requests, contact emails, and routine operational data related to the use of cocoonlab.ai.

Who is responsible

Cocoon Lab is the operator of this site and the company responsible for handling information shared through it.

How to reach us

Email rashid@cocoonlab.ai for any privacy or data-handling question.

Information we may receive

  • Your name, email address, company, role, and project details when you contact us or request a demo.
  • Operational and security information generated by hosting and infrastructure providers, such as request logs and performance data.
  • Any additional information you choose to share in correspondence with Cocoon Lab.

How we use that information

  • To respond to your inquiry, continue a conversation you initiated, or schedule a product discussion.
  • To evaluate whether Cocoon is a fit for your team, project, or studio workflow.
  • To maintain the security, integrity, and reliability of the website and related communications.
  • To improve how we explain the product and communicate with prospective teams.

Retention

We keep business correspondence for as long as it is reasonably useful to manage the relationship, respond to follow-up questions, and maintain records, unless a longer retention period is required by law or justified by legitimate operational need.

Sharing

Cocoon Lab does not sell personal information. Information may be processed by service providers involved in hosting, email, infrastructure, analytics, or security, but only to the extent needed to operate the website and support legitimate business communication.

Security

We use reasonable technical and operational measures to protect information shared through this website. No internet-based system can promise absolute security, so if you need to share especially sensitive material, contact us first and we can agree on an appropriate channel.

Your choices

You can email rashid@cocoonlab.ai to ask what information you have shared with Cocoon Lab, request correction of inaccurate details, or request deletion where appropriate.
