# Cocoon Lab Press Kit

Official brand assets for Cocoon Lab and Cocoon.

Use this kit for editorial coverage, partner announcements, social posts, event materials, and approved product references. For questions or unusual use cases, contact rashid@cocoonlab.ai.

## Contents

- `01_Logos/`: Cocoon logo and Cocoon Lab logotype in SVG, PNG, PDF, and EPS.
- `02_Glyphs/`: Cocoon glyph assets in standard and chamfered versions.
- `03_Lockups/`: Horizontal and stacked brand lockups for light and dark backgrounds.
- `04_Color/`: Color tokens, palettes, and design-system color files.
- `05_Typography/`: Typography direction for brand use.
- `06_Social/`: Open Graph, title card, square, and LinkedIn cover assets.
- `07_Favicons/`: Browser favicon and app icon files.
- `08_Imagery/`: Website imagery and team portraits.

## Typography

- Display and headlines: Outfit
- Body, labels, and supporting text: Plus Jakarta Sans

## Usage Notes

- Keep the logo clear, legible, and unmodified.
- Use the provided Ink assets on light backgrounds and Ivory or OnInk assets on dark backgrounds.
- Do not stretch, recolor, rotate, distort, outline, or add effects to the logo.
- Do not place the logo on busy imagery without enough contrast.
- Use SVG or EPS for production artwork when possible.
- Use PNG assets for web, presentations, and social layouts.
