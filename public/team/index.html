Founding context

Built from practice, not abstraction.

The company emerged from years of work alongside creative professionals, architectural firms, and community organizations in Montreal.

That background keeps Cocoon focused on real decisions, real constraints, and real studio workflows instead of generic automation.

Shared position

Architecture remains authored by people.

Cocoon exists to support architects and development teams by making early-stage information clearer, more structured, and easier to use.

The goal is not generation for its own sake. It is stronger judgment, better collaboration, and better environments.
