The founding team behind Cocoon

Cocoon Lab was founded by Rashid Mushkani, Hugo Berard, and Shin Koseki. Together they are building Cocoon to bring clearer design intelligence to the earliest stages of architecture.

Founding team

Rashid Mushkani, Hugo Berard, and Shin Koseki founded Cocoon Lab in 2025.

What they are building

Cocoon helps teams understand site, cost, carbon, code, and visuals before design is fixed.

Grounding

The work emerged from years of engagement with architecture, creative practice, and community organizations in Montreal.

What anchors the team

The work at Cocoon Lab is rooted in the idea that architecture should remain authored by people. Cocoon exists to make early-stage information clearer, more structured, and more usable.

What the team is focused on

Cocoon is being shaped around the earliest project decisions: site, cost, carbon, code, and visual understanding before a direction is fixed.
