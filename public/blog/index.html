Cocoon Lab in partnership with Mila

Published: April 13, 2026

Mila brings research depth, rigor, and a serious culture of responsible AI. That context matters as Cocoon Lab builds Cocoon to help teams understand site, cost, carbon, and regulatory signals earlier.
